# 갈등 해결을 위한 Norder 표기법

## Norder Notation

Defination of Norder Operator
x▪︎y ≡ y▪︎x

Defination of Norder String
1. 「x y」≡ x▪︎y
2. 「x y z」≡ x▪︎y▪︎z
3. ...

## Pronunciation of Norder Notation

0. read `「□」` by Pronunciation of `[norder □]`
1. [norder □ ○] ≡ [norder ○ □]
2. [norder □ ○ ◇] ≡ [norder □ ◇ ○] ≡ [norder ○ □ ◇] ≡ [norder ○ ◇ □] ≡ [norder ◇ □ ○] ≡ [norder ◇ ○ □]
3. ...

## example

「日 韓」의 뜻은 「韓 日」임.
